Who Are We

Neokecci Multiconcepts Company Limited, is a Nigerian based company founded in 2005 but incorporated in 2010, and aimed at sustainable development and community transformation in the Niger Delta region. We support multinational companies who wish to encourage youth and women growth, and development. We support these multinationals in setting up enterprise development, community projects and impact assessment.

We are also accreedited members of the Shell LiveWIRE training team, having mentored over 1,600 entrepreneurs and executed over 242 rural projects, worth over $10m in Niger Delta communities. Currently, we are running VIBES, a business training and mentorship program with NLNG, contributing to the infrastructural and econimic development of the Niger Delta

Do You Know??

Neokecci Multiconcepts Company Limited is currently implementing the Vocational, Innovation, Business, and Empowerment Scheme (VIBES), a business management training, mentorship and coaching programme powered by Nigeria LNG Limited (NLNG) 2024. It is a programme aimed at offering training, mentorship and networking opportunities to small and large business owners. Checkout their website to participate.
